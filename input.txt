My Youth Romantic Comedy Is Wrong, As I Expected or Yahari Ore no Seishun Rabukome wa Machigatteiru, abbreviated as OreGairu and Hamachi, and also known as My Teen Romantic Comedy SNAFU, is a Japanese light novel series written by Wataru Watari and illustrated by Ponkan8. The series follows Hachiman Hikigaya, a pessimistic, closeminded, and realistic teen, who is forced by his teacher to join the school's service club and work with two girls with issues of their own. They offer help and advice to others while dealing with their inner conflicts.
